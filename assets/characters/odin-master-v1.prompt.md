Lighting/mood: Bright, cheerful, high-key animation lighting. Affectionate, clever, calm, lightly skeptical.

Scene/backdrop: Perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background for local background removal. The background must be one uniform color with no shadows, gradients, texture, reflections, floor plane, or lighting variation.
Constraints: Preserve Odin's identity and distinctive facial proportions across Images 2 through 5. Crisp opaque illustrated character edges and generous padding. Do not use #00ff00 anywhere in the subject. No cast shadow, contact shadow, glow, text, logos, watermark, props, other people, extra objects, scenery, extra fingers, or extra limbs.
